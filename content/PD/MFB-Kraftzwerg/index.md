The Kraftzwerg is a tiny semi-modular device. It uses the basic Minimoog design (3 oscillators, 1 filter) but adds a bunch of patch points.

-   **Patches**: 13
-   **Formats:** EXS24, Kontakt & SFZ
-   **Approx Size:** 1GB
